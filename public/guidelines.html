Tcash User Guidelines

Last updated: July 2026

Tcash is a World mini app that lets verified World App users buy and sell WLD and USDC with Kenyan Shilling (KES) settlement over mobile money. These guidelines explain how Tcash works, what we expect from users, and what you can expect from us.

Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions

1. Who can use Tcash

Age requirement Required

You must be at least 18 years old. Tcash is not available to minors.

World App account Required

You must have a genuine, active World App account and sign in using World Wallet Auth. Shared, borrowed, or fictitious accounts are not permitted.

Kenya mobile-money access

Tcash settles in KES via M-Pesa (Safaricom) or Airtel Money. You need a registered Kenyan mobile-money number to receive sell payouts or referral rewards.

Lawful use Required

You are responsible for ensuring your use of Tcash complies with the laws and regulations that apply to you in your jurisdiction.

2. How orders work

Buy orders (M-Pesa → WLD or USDC)

  1. Enter the KES amount you want to pay. Tcash shows you a live quote with the applicable fee before you submit.
  2. Pay the exact amount to the Tcash M-Pesa PayBill shown on screen — include your unique order reference as the account number.
  3. Submit your M-Pesa transaction code after payment. The admin operator will verify the payment and release the equivalent WLD or USDC to your World wallet.

Sell orders (WLD or USDC → KES)

  1. Enter the crypto amount you want to sell. Tcash shows a live KES payout quote with fee before you submit.
  2. Approve the World Pay transaction inside World App. Tcash confirms the on-chain payment with the World Developer Portal.
  3. After the admin operator reviews and confirms receipt, KES is sent to your saved M-Pesa payout number.

Settlement times

All orders are manually reviewed. Typical settlement is within a few hours during business hours. If an order is not settled within 24 hours, contact support via WhatsApp or email.

Rates and fees

Exchange rates are updated from live market data. A per-coin fee applies to every order and is shown in the quote before you confirm. Rates can move between quote and settlement, especially in volatile markets. The settled rate is the one active when the admin processes the order.

3. Your responsibilities

Accurate details

Provide a correct M-Pesa payout number, accurate transaction codes, and truthful order information. Errors in your payout number can result in funds being sent to the wrong account.

Only your funds

Only use funds and wallets that you legally own and are entitled to transact with. Do not transact on behalf of a third party without their explicit consent.

Single account

Use one World account per user. Operating multiple accounts to exploit the referral program or any Tcash feature is prohibited.

No fraudulent activity Prohibited

Do not submit false payment codes, reverse payments after crypto is released, or engage in any form of fraud, deception, or manipulation of the order process.

No illegal activity Prohibited

Tcash must not be used for money laundering, financing illegal activity, circumventing sanctions, or any other unlawful purpose.

4. Referral program rules

Genuine referrals only

Referral rewards are for inviting real, new World App users who trade on Tcash. Self-referrals, fake accounts, and reward manipulation are prohibited and will result in account suspension.

Reward eligibility

Base reward (KES 30) is credited when a referred user completes their first trade. Milestone bonuses (KES 100 at 6 activated referrals, KES 150 at 10) require reaching the milestone threshold with genuine trades.

Reward claims

Claims are reviewed manually and settled to your saved M-Pesa payout number. Tcash reserves the right to withhold rewards that appear fraudulent or manipulated.

5. Order disputes and refunds

Contact Tcash support as soon as possible if:

  • A buy order is not settled after you have paid M-Pesa.
  • A sell order KES payout has not arrived within 24 hours.
  • You believe there is an error in the amount settled.

Tcash will investigate and, where possible, refund or reverse the order. Once an order is marked completed and funds have been sent, reversals may not be possible. Contact support before placing a duplicate order.

6. Notifications

Tcash uses World App push notifications to alert you when an order is placed, reviewed, or completed. Notifications require your explicit permission inside World App. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in Profile.

7. Account suspension and termination

Tcash may suspend or terminate access to your account if you:

  • Violate these guidelines, the Terms & Conditions, or applicable laws.
  • Submit fraudulent payment information or attempt to defraud the service.
  • Abuse the referral program with fake accounts or self-referrals.
  • Engage in or facilitate money laundering or illegal transactions.

In cases of suspension, any pending orders will be reviewed individually and settled or reversed at Tcash's discretion.

8. Limitations of service

  • Manual settlement — All orders are processed by a human operator. Settlement is not instant or automated.
  • Trade limits — Buy orders: KES 600–20,000. Sell orders: minimum USD equivalent of $1. Limits may change without prior notice.
  • Kenya only — Mobile-money settlement is available via M-Pesa Kenya and Airtel Kenya. International settlement is not currently supported.
  • Single admin desk — One operator manages all orders. High volume periods may cause delays.

9. Risk disclosure

Cryptocurrency values can change significantly in a short time. The KES amount you receive for a sell order depends on the rate active when your order is processed, which may differ from the quote shown at submission. Tcash does not guarantee any particular exchange rate or payout amount. Trading crypto carries financial risk, including the possibility of receiving less than you expected. Only trade amounts you can afford to lose.
