Tcash Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tcash ("Tcash", "we", "us") is a World mini app that lets people in Kenya buy and sell WLD or USDC and settle in Kenyan Shillings (KES) over mobile money. Every order is reviewed and settled by a human operator. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • World account details — your World wallet address and username, obtained when you sign in with World App Wallet Auth.
  • Mobile-money number — the M-Pesa / mobile-money phone number you enter to receive or make payments.
  • Order details — the asset, amount, KES value, timestamps, and blockchain or mobile-money transaction references for orders you create.
  • Notification permission — only if you choose to enable World App notifications.

We do not collect your private keys, passwords, government IDs, or card numbers, and we do not ask you to enter them.

How we use your information

  • To process, review, and settle the buy and sell orders you place.
  • To contact you about the status of your orders.
  • To send notifications you have opted into.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse, and to keep records as required by law.

How we share information

We share order information only as needed to settle your order — for example with the operator who completes the mobile-money payout or releases crypto, and with mobile-money providers used for settlement. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep order and account records for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet legal, accounting, and anti-fraud obligations, after which they are deleted or anonymised.

Your choices and rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may turn notifications off at any time in World App settings. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Tcash is not intended for anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
